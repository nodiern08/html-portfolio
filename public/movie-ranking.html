The Best Movies Acording to Nodier

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Jurrasic Park

I kid you not; I've watched this movie more than 30 times.

I love dinosaurs, and as a child, I'd rewatch this movie all the time.
My sisters still remind me often how I would put on the movie every night before bed
and run out of my room frightened during this particular "scary" scene.

This movie holds a special place in my heart and I'll love it forever.

2. Interstellar

Okay, so I love anything related to aliens and outer space.

I find the idea of "Are we alone?" fascinating, and the sheer size of our universe is mind-boggling.
Albeit there are no aliens in this movie, that does not deter me from believing this movie is a masterpiece.
Interstellar has impressive visuals, acting, and a crazy representation of how the universe could behave.
From drama, love, and space-warping ideas, this movie has it all.

3. Signs

Last but not least, Signs.

Are we alone? I'd like to believe we are not.
What are the chances that we are the only living beings in our universe?
Who knows, but there's a good chance there's life out there.

Isn't that exciting?

To some, it may be, like myself. But what if these extraterrestrials are not the happy little grays we imagined?
What if they were hostile? And how would the world react to an invasion?

Signs showcase the horror in the idea of being in the presence of a hostile being not from this world.
In this movie, you will watch a family live out that horror fantasy and do whatever it takes to keep themselves safe.

I have Signs on my 3rd spot because of the suspenseful upcoming of what a hostile alien invasion could look like,
without the dramatic catastrophe of spaceships flying everywhere destroying everything in sight.

Instead, it's a slow-paced thriller that builds up right in the comfort of your very own home.
